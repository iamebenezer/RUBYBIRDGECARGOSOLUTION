Who We Are ?

Logistics services

RBSC is an online Truckers-Marketplace where shippers (anyone who has goods/loads) post their shipment and get bids from truckers/transporters nationwide.

Our Company provides a wide range of alternative prices or bids from verified truckers/transporters on the platform, catering for all load sizes ranging from parcels to full truck load (FTL), RBSC is currently the partner of choice to e-Commerce businesses in the country as well as several FMCG distributors, and retail chain stores in Nigeria.

RBSC also provides a platform for truckers/transporters to get loads and shipment request on the go with notifications via mobile apps, sms, and emails ensuring truckers get continuous streams of revenue. More so, other individuals can benefit from the scheme by earning referral commissions as Agents/Referrers. This is in line with the vision of creating employment opportunities for individuals who are yet to be fully employed, or extra income for the employed, ensuring that everyone is a winner.
