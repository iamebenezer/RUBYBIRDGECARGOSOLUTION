Everything Is Okay In The End. If it's Not Okay, Then It's Not The End (Video)
  • Ebenezer Abegunde
  • 21

Right now the worst is happening, and you feel like Everything is going to fall apart. It might be your fault, but that’s not very important. What’s important is that you remember that where you are is temporary, and tomorrow is going to be better.

Challenge yourself to believe this, and hold onto the lightness that comes from letting go. Let go completely. Remember that life is temporary, and this challenge is just a blip on the radar.

Close your eyes and try to imagine it. If you can’t, then think about what you wish it would look like; that time when everything is fine. If this proves impossible, then focus on beautiful things: laughter in the playground, winning a raffle, having a whole row to yourself on a red-eye flight. Practice inserting yourself into that list of beautiful things. You will be okay. Everything will be okay in the end. if it's not okay, then it's not the end.
